Communication Test

Let's test the communication setting. 1- Put your headphones on.
2- Choose your audio and video input.
3- Click start.
4- Talk.
Can you see and hear yourself correctly? Can you hear your voice clearly? Is the sound quality excelent and the microphone working?

If you can see and hear yourself correctly and in good quality, it means that you can participate in the experiment. Remember the configuration that you used you will be asked to use this after.

If you can't see or hear anything after pressing start or if there is "ERROR" displayed, then it means that there is a compatibility problem with your internet connection and you are not allowed to participate in the experiment. In that case, please return your prolific submission with code Sett_ERROR. Don't worry you will be compnensated for the time you took setting up the experiment.
